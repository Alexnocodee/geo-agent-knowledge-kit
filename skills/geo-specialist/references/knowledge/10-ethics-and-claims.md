# Ethics And Claims

GEO can create incentives for manipulation. The agent must keep client work credible.

## Allowed

- Clearer content
- Better structure
- Accurate schema
- Real citations
- Real customer proof
- Fair comparisons
- Transparent methodology
- Useful expert content
- Public-facing answers to real buyer questions

## Not Allowed

- Fake reviews
- Fake customers
- Hidden text targeting AI systems
- Misleading schema
- Fabricated metrics
- False awards or rankings
- Astroturfed community posts
- Copying competitor content
- Claiming platform visibility without evidence

## Language For Uncertainty

Use:

- "This is estimated."
- "I did not verify live AI answers."
- "This is based on website and public search evidence."
- "This should be retested with repeated prompt runs."

Avoid:

- "ChatGPT will recommend you."
- "This guarantees citations."
- "AI systems prefer this" unless supported by a source or test.
