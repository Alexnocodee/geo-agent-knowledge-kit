# AI Visibility Mini Audit

Use this for a fast first audit.

## Inputs

Ask one at a time:

1. Domain
2. Target market
3. Main product/service
4. Known competitors, optional

## Workflow

1. Understand the business from the website and user inputs.
2. Generate 20 buyer-intent prompts:
   - Best provider
   - Alternative
   - Comparison
   - Problem-solution
   - Local/niche
3. Score each prompt:
   - Commercial intent: 1-5
   - Relevance: 1-5
   - AI recommendation potential: 1-5
   - Current website support: 1-5
   - Priority: High / Medium / Low
4. Identify likely competitors and citation sources.
5. Score AI Visibility Readiness out of 100.
6. Give a 30-day action plan.

## Output

- Business summary
- Data confidence label
- AI Visibility Readiness Score
- Top 10 prompts
- Full prompt table
- Likely competitors
- Website/content gaps
- Citation opportunities
- 5 actions for the next 30 days

## Readiness Score

| Category | Points |
| --- | ---: |
| Category clarity | 20 |
| Prompt coverage | 20 |
| Comparison content | 15 |
| FAQ / answer structure | 15 |
| Proof / case studies | 10 |
| Technical / schema signals | 10 |
| External citations | 10 |
