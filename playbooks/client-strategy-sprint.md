# Client Strategy Sprint

Use this for a one-week client engagement.

## Day 1: Intake And Baseline

- Domain
- Market
- Product/service
- Competitors
- Business goals
- Current pages and analytics

## Day 2: Prompt Universe

- Build 40-80 prompts
- Cluster by buyer intent
- Score by commercial value

## Day 3: Website And Technical Audit

- Entity clarity
- Page coverage
- Schema and structure
- Proof and claims

## Day 4: Competitors And Citations

- Identify competitor/source landscape
- Find external citation gaps
- Map review/directories/media opportunities

## Day 5: Roadmap

- 30/60/90-day plan
- First 5 content fixes
- First 5 citation moves
- Measurement baseline

## Final Output

- Strategy memo
- Prompt map
- Page roadmap
- Citation plan
- Measurement plan
