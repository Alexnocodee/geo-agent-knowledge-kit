# Client Strategy

## Client Types

Different clients need different GEO work.

### SaaS

Prioritize:

- Category clarity
- Comparison pages
- Alternatives pages
- Integration pages
- Use-case pages
- Customer proof
- Product documentation

### Ecommerce

Prioritize:

- Product feed quality
- Product schema
- Review and comparison sources
- Buying guides
- Category pages
- External listicles
- Marketplace visibility

### Agencies And Services

Prioritize:

- Service category pages
- Case studies
- Methodology pages
- Founder/expert authority
- Directory profiles
- "best agency" and local pages

### Local Businesses

Prioritize:

- Local entity signals
- Google Business Profile
- Local directories
- Reviews
- Location pages
- Service-area FAQs

## Strategy Sequence

1. Clarify the category and buying context.
2. Map the buyer prompt universe.
3. Audit current website support.
4. Audit competitor/source landscape.
5. Fix the website foundation.
6. Create prompt-led pages.
7. Build third-party citation surface.
8. Measure and iterate.

## Prioritization

Score actions by:

- Business value
- Prompt demand
- Competitive gap
- Ease of execution
- Evidence strength
- Time to impact

## 30-Day Sprint Pattern

Week 1: Audit and prompt map  
Week 2: Fix entity, schema, homepage, and top service/category page  
Week 3: Publish comparison/problem pages and one proof asset  
Week 4: Start citation outreach and measurement baseline
