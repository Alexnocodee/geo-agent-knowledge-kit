# Citation Opportunity Plan

Use this to make the brand more citeable outside its own website.

## Inputs

- Domain
- Category
- Market
- Competitors
- Existing mentions
- Proof assets available

## Workflow

1. Identify sources AI systems may cite for this category.
2. Group sources by type.
3. Find gaps where competitors appear and the client does not.
4. Define the asset needed for each source.
5. Prioritize by relevance, authority, feasibility, and buyer intent.

## Source Types

- Review platforms
- Directories
- Industry publications
- Comparison/listicle sites
- Partner pages
- Integration marketplaces
- Podcasts/webinars
- Communities
- Original research pages
- Analyst/report pages
